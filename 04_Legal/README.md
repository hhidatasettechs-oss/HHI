# HHI Legal Frameworks (Public Index)

This directory contains **licensing frameworks, legal references, and compliance artifacts**
used by the Hollow House Institute (HHI) to govern datasets, code, and derived works.

⚠️ **Important**
- This folder is **public-safe**.
- No private contracts, personal identity materials, or confidential legal documents are included.
- Some licenses reference external or future commercial agreements not distributed here.

---

## Directory Purpose

The `04_Legal` directory serves as the **canonical public index** for:

- Dataset licensing terms
- Open-source license inheritance
- Third-party license attribution
- Governance scaffolding for HHI systems

It is designed for:
- Auditors
- Enterprise reviewers
- Legal & compliance teams
- Open-source consumers

---

## What You’ll Find Here

### Core Licenses
- `LICENSE`, `LICENSE.txt`
- `LICENSE_APACHE`, `LICENSE_MIT`, `LICENSE_BSD`, `LICENSE_GPL`, `LICENSE_ISC`
- `LICENSE_THIRD_PARTY`

### License Variants & Bundles
- Browser, server, extension, and vendor license bundles
- Copilot, Node, and extension-related licenses
- Indexed license manifests

### Documentation
- `LICENSES_INDEX.md` – master index of all licenses
- `README_LICENSE_GUIDE.md` – guidance on interpreting license scope

### Metadata
- `*.metadata.json` files for traceability, audit tooling, and automation

---

## What Is Explicitly Not Included

- Private contracts
- Client agreements
- Identity or likeness protection documents
- Commercial-only license texts
- Negotiated enterprise terms

Those materials are maintained in **private repositories or offline vaults**.

---

## Usage Notes

- Licenses apply **only to assets explicitly distributed in this repository**.
- Presence of a license does **not** imply automatic grant for all HHI intellectual property.
- Always refer to dataset-level passports and README files for asset-specific terms.
